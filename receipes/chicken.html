Chicken Kabob

Receipe

Makes approx. 4 servings.

Ingredients

Chicken

Approx. 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast

Marinade Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 3/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • 2-4 tablespoons of Greek Seasoning

Greek Seasoning

  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 2 teaspoons basil
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Directions

  1. Combine all marinade ingredients into a gallon sized ziplock back or bowl and mix thoroughly.
  2. Using cutting board and knife. Cut chicken into approx. 1" cubes.
  3. Add chicken to marinade and mix until all chicken is coated on all sides.
  4. Let marinade refrigerate for approx. 30 minutes to 4 hours.
