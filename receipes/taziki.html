Taziki

Receipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 cup fineliy chopped or grated cucumber
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes
  • Pita and vegetables, for serving (optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. In a medium bowl, combine yougurt, oil, and lemon juice. Add cucumber, garlic, and dill; season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper.

  2. Step 2

    3. Mix until well blended

  3. Step 3

    4. Serve with pita and vegetables (if using)

  4. Step 4

    5. Make Ahead: Taziki can be made up to 4 days ahead. Transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate until needed.
