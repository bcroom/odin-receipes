Zucchini Lasagna Receipe

Receipe

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive oil
  • 1 Large onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 pound ground beef (extra lean)
  • 2 tablespoons Italian Seasoning
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes

Zucchini Lasagna

  • 3 large zucchini
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese (grated)

Instructions

  1. Meat Sauce: Add the olive oil to a large skillet and heat over medium heat. Add onion and cook for a couple of minutes until onion is soft. Add garlic and ground beef, breaking the ground beef with whatever tool is available.

  2. Cook until the meat is no longer pink. Add Italian Seasoning and crushed tomatoes, stir and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.

  3. Prepare Zucchini: While the sauce is simmering you can prepare the zucchini, by either using a knife or a mandoline to slice them. Slice the zucchini approx. 1/8" thick.

  4. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.

  5. Assembly: Using a 9 X 13 baking dish. Start with a layer of zucchini slices. Top the layer with a thin layer of meat sauce folowed by mozzarella and ricotta cheese. Next repeat until you run out of ingredients. Make sure to fiinish layering with the meat sauce and top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.

  6. Bake: Place the uncooked lasagna into the oven and bake for approx. 40-50 minutses or until the top is starting to turn golden and cheese is bubbly.

  7. Remove cooked lasagna form the oven, garnish with parsley if desired, and allow to cool for 5-10 minutes.Slice, serve and enjoy.

Notes

Optional Step: to remove excess water from the zucchini, lay slices out over sheets of paper towels, sprinkle salt over the slices and place another paper towel over the top to absorb excess water. Repeat as necessary.
